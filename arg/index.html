A Rock Game

Alessandro Bruni

Department of Computer Science, University of California Santa Cruz

Abstract:

Genetic Algorithms have been successfully applied to music generation and have even passed the Turing test for AIs in jazz jam sessions. This work tries to apply them in the field of video games, expanding the genre of music games and improving the player's creativity.
